What is this game about? This is a top-down perspective stealth game. You need to get in and get out hopefully without being seen. Your main goal is to acquire the coveted giant ruby in each level. You do this by evading guards and security cameras while using all of the tools at your disposal.

SNEAK AROUND!

Guards patrol around the level! Guards have a set patrol route in which they patrol endlessly. If you happen to be spotted by a guard, they will chase you for awhile. If you happen to evade them long enough, they will get bored and return to their patrol route. But if you happen to get caught, it's off to jail for you.

Don't get spotted by the security cameras! Entering the security cameras line of sight will alert every guard in the map to your position. They will leave their positions and go to the place you were spotted.

TOOLS OF THE TRADE

There are a variety of tools at your disposal! Tools are scattered about most levels and can help you get an edge up on the competition. Simply walk over a tool and press space to use it. You can cycle between tools by pressing q and e. Tools break after one use so use them wisely!

Coins: These are scattered around most levels. Collecting them will increase your score. They don't really DO anything per se, but bragging rights are always fun.
Keys: These come in a variety of colors and are strewn across the map for some reason. Use them to unlock locks with the same color.
Banana Peels: The fresh remains of someone's lunch. Drop the peel somewhere and wait for a guard to slip on it. They'll fall down and be incapacitated for a few seconds.
Jammers: Issues a targeted jamming frequency in a wide area. Use this to scramble the visual sensors of every security camera on the map for a few seconds.
Pocket Watches: Don't people have wrist watches nowadays? I mean wrist watches are less likely to fall off of your person, which evidently happened to this pocket watch. Use this to stop time for a few seconds.
LEVELS

There are currently 10 levels in the game. Each level is more challenging than the last. You are given a score based on your speed, coins collected, and if you were seen by the guards.

IMPLEMENTATION

This is a Java project and was developed in Eclipse. It uses the JIG library which is essentially a modified lwjgl.
