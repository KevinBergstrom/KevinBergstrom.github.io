What is this game about? This is a game about hitting a ball with a paddle. I'm sure you've seen the classic "Breakout" on the Atari. Well this is essentially an upgraded version of that. It features a variety of power ups, plenty of levels, and bricks that fight back.

POWER-UPS

Power-ups spawn at random intervals or when you destroy certain brick types. They start high up on the screen and will gradually float down to the bottom. Once you collect one, its effect is used immediately.

Accelerate: Increases the speed at which the paddle moves.
Heal: Heals the player for 1 health point.
Luck: Increases the rate at which power-ups spawn.
Forcefield: Provides immunity to projectile damage for a short amount of time.
Slow: Decreases the speed a which the ball moves.
Damage: Increases the damage of the ball.
BRICKS

Bricks are the main obstacle you have to overcome. They come in all shapes and sizes (from 1 to 4 units tall and 1 to 4 units wide). You will win the level once all bricks are destroyed. Most bricks spawn projectiles or affect you when they are destroyed.

Basic Brick: The default brick. It doesn't have any special effects.
Speed Brick: Increases the ball speed when you damage it.
Inversion Brick: Inverts your controls when you damage it.
Power-up Brick: Spawns a random power-up when you damage it.
Radioactive Brick: Spawns a damaging projectile from where you damaged it. The projectile falls directly downward and accelerates.
Shooting Brick: Fires a damaging projectile at you when you damage it. This projectile is unaffected by gravity.
LEVELS

There are currently 7 levels in this game. Each level is more challenging than the last. You are given a score based on how long it takes to complete the level, amount of damage taken, and amount of power-ups used.

IMPLEMENTATION

This is a Java project and was developed in Eclipse. It uses the JIG library which is essentially a modified lwjgl.
